Biography

I am currently an Assistant Researcher at Zhongguancun Laboratory. From 2020 to 2021, I was with Networking Platform Department, Tencent and Theory Laboratory, Huawei Hong Kong Research Center. I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong in 2020, advised by Dr. Hong Xu. Before joining CityU, I got my B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in building intelligent and secure networking systems. My current focus is network operations and management, routing security, and LLM systems.
